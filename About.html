About me

A designer, yes — but also a noodle enthusiast, an occasional photographer, and a firm believer that good work comes from real curiosity. Here's a little of the person behind the portfolio.

Hi, I'm Betty👋🏻

I'm based in Singapore, currently designing citizen-facing services at GovTech. Before that, I was in fintech, designing for the private market investments space.

Outside of work, you'll usually find me chasing good light with a camera, hunting down the city's best bowl of noodles, or slowly over-watering my plants. I think the best designers are just curious people who happen to make things.

A bit more

How I got here.

I didn't take the straight path into design — and I'm glad. I started out curious about how money moves and why people trust (or don't trust) the apps they use every day. That curiosity pulled me into fintech, then into the public sector, where the stakes are different: when you design for everyone, you can't design for the average.

These days I care most about clarity, accessibility, and making things that feel calm to use. The work that makes me proudest is usually invisible — the friction someone never hit because we caught it first.
