Betty Goh
UX Designer · GovTech, Singapore

I design calm, human products for the messy real world — and I'm a little obsessed with the parts nobody thanks you for.

Today I design citizen-facing services at GovTech, where “the user” is genuinely everyone — every age, every language, every level of patience. It's the most humbling brief I've ever had.

Before this, I spent my fintech years learning that trust is built in the boring moments: the error message, the empty state, the 2 a.m. edge case. Now I bring that same stubborn care to things millions of people quietly rely on.
