Enhancing the product discovery experience for confident decision-making.

Enhancing the product discovery experience for confident decision-making. ADDX is an investment platform offering access to private market investments and alternatives in fractions, made possible with blockchain. This project improved product discovery across both the app and web platforms.

RoleProduct Designer
Team1 PD · 1 UXR · 3 Eng · 1 PM · 1 Data Analyst
TimelineJul – Sep 2023
ToolsFigma · Maze
Our focus

The product listing page — the gateway to becoming an investor.

The product listing page is where users discover investment products, so it's the essential entry point that turns a curious sign-up into an active investor.

Background

New users weren't transitioning into active users.

An active user is someone who invests within 30 days of signing up. Too many new users were stalling before that first investment — and the product listing page was a notable hurdle.

Because the product listing page serves as the gateway for users to discover investment products, stakeholders unanimously agreed that optimising this crucial funnel point would yield the most impactful results.

Project goal

Improve the product listing page by enhancing navigation, filter functionality and the information hierarchy of the invest card — and in turn, lift user activation rates.

Success metrics

1

Listing → detail CTR

Increase click-through rate from the product listing to the product detail page.

2

Filter usage

Increase the click-through rate of the exposed filters on app and web.

3

Watchlist engagement

Increase watchlist engagement of investment products.

4

Time on page

Decrease time spent on the product listing page.

Research

Listening to investors and the sales team.

Together with the UX Researcher, we conducted user interviews with five investors and members of the sales team. Four insights stood out:

1

Listing page usability

Users struggle to find available products and compare asset classes.

2

Card information hierarchy

Users find the image uninformative and prefer more relevant product details.

3

Past offerings display

Users question the relevance of matured offerings since they can no longer invest in them.

4

Rigid offering setup

The sales team wants more flexibility to configure product information for better selling.

UX audit

Auditing the old design.

I conducted a UX audit of the old design, identifying pain points and potential areas of improvement.

1

Hidden filters

Filters are only accessible through the filter icon — fewer than 5% of users use them to find products.

2

No results header

There's no header showing the number of available offerings.

3

Disproportionate cards

The image-to-content ratio of each invest card is off, and information hierarchy needs improvement.

4

Cramped first view

In the first view, users see only one invest card and part of the next product's image.

Target users

Targeting self-directed, proficient investors for high ROI.

Focusing on confident, self-directed investors was expected to yield the highest return on the redesign effort.

Process · Ideation

A Crazy 8s workshop across departments.

I ran a Crazy 8s design workshop to facilitate cross-department collaboration. Product Designers, Product Managers and business stakeholders all contributed fresh ideas.

Process · Concept testing

Internal concept testing on Maze.

I ran internal concept testing on Maze with stakeholders, presenting two design variations to gather feedback. The goal was to assess how effectively each conveyed key information — making sure the final design aligned with both stakeholder needs and user expectations.

Final solution · Product listing

Better categorisation, visible filters, clearer cards.

1

Exposed filters

Increased visibility of filters by exposing some of them just below the tabs.

2

Section headers

Headers to indicate what products are being shown.

3

‘New’ tag

Addition of a 'new' tag to inform users of new investment products.

4

Admin control

Customisable product details from the admin portal.

Final solution · Filters & past offerings

A clearer filter sheet, and past offerings that build confidence.

Impact

We surpassed our target for listing-to-detail conversion. 🥳

The redesigned product listing page beat its goal for conversion rate from product listing to product detail page.
